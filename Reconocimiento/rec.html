Steve Jobs

"Innovation distinguishes a leader from a follower."

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur, industrial designer, business magnate, media proprietor, and investor. He was the co-founder and CEO of Apple and the largest individual shareholder of The Walt Disney Company.

Steve Jobs revolutionized technology and contemporary culture through his work at Apple, Pixar, and his focus on design and innovation.

Chronology

  • 1976

    Foundation of Apple

    Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne founded Apple Computer in a garage.

  • 1985

    Apple Event

    Jobs leaves Apple after internal struggles with management.

  • 1997

    Return and leadership

    Jobs returns to Apple and begins the strategy that will lead to products like the iMac and iPod.
